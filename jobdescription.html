UI/UX Designer - 2 openings

This is a full-time position for a UI/UX Designer with some years of experience in user interface design for the web, mobile apps . Salary ranges from Rs. 50,000 to 100,000.

 Job Location Kathmandu, Uk
 Job Type: Full Time
 vacant: 2 Persons
 salary: 50,000 - 100000
 Skills: Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma, Sketch,

We are looking for an experienced user interface and user experience designer to join our team. We are looking for self-managed people who can work without constant supervision.

Responsibilities

We want people who are motivated to design eye-catching, accessible and simple interfaces for the web and mobile. You will be responsible for one or more of the following tasks depending on your skills and prior experience.

  • Research and reimagine the overall look and feel of our existing products
  • Design the user interface of our mobile apps, web apps and games
  • Ensure our products look great on all screen sizes and variations
  • Work with the graphics designer to create screenshots and promotional material
  • Work closely with the designers to develop UI design guidelines for the company
  • Work closely with the developers to ensure high quality experience for our users
  • Work closely with the motion designer for creating in-app animations and transitions
  • Design, update and maintain our standard design guidelines
  • Help the junior developers learn the best practices of ui/ux design

Minimum Requirements:

If you have at least a few of the following skills, you can apply for this position. You will be assigned the best role based on your prior experience.

  • Excellent knowledge of a UI design tool like Figma, Sketch, Penpot or Adobe XD
  • A diverse portfolio showcasing different variants of your work and skills
  • Intermediate knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and similar tools
  • Capable of rapid prototyping and iteration based on feedback
  • Capable of learning new tools as required by the role

Preferred Qualities:
