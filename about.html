About Digital Yeti

Digital Yeti is an innovative, affordable and reliable IT Software House aimed at Small to Medium businesses with a view to enable and empower them through exceptional IT services. Our motto is to link the IT needs from the businesses with the talent pool from Nepal and create a win-win environment for both parties. The idea of providing IT services from Nepal is rooted on our core value of developing and nurturing talent in Nepal who will surprise and delight the customers in and outside of Nepal. The owners have over 2 decades of working and providing IT services in developed countries and bring their proven experience and exposure of delivering a small project to multi-million pounds programmes.

Why Digtial Yeti ?

There is no doubt when it comes to IT offshoring, India will probably spring to everybody's mind. However, we believe that there are a number of other locations which have been ignored by many organisations or customers and perhaps missing out on the talent as well as economic benefit of multi-location. One such location is Nepal, which is on a similar timeline in terms of time zone for offshoring compared to India and boasts an excellent supply of superior talent. Due to the popularity of India neighbouring countries like Nepal has been completely overlooked. Growing crop of top talent and untapped market of skill in Nepal can certainly fulfil the IT services.

Why Nepal ?

  • Fresh and untouched, very little-known talent market = less competition for talent.
  • Freedom to shape the skillset according to your need = purchase what you need.
  • Favourable FX rate compared to local currency = economically superior to other alternative location.
  • Fluent English speaking talent with convenient time-zone = a ready made workforce for your organisation.
  • Free market and generous regulations = easy to do business with.
