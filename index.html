Digital Yeti

Digital Yeti is an innovative, affordable and reliable IT Software House aimed at Small to Medium businesses with a view to enable and empower them through exceptional IT services. Our motto is to link the IT needs from the businesses with the talent pool from Nepal and create a win-win environment for both parties. The idea of providing IT services from Nepal is rooted on our core value of developing and nurturing talent in Nepal who will surprise and delight the customers in and outside of Nepal. The owners have over 2 decades of working and providing IT services in developed countries and bring their proven experience and exposure of delivering a small project to multi-million pounds programmes.

Why Digital Yeti ?

There is no doubt when it comes to IT offshoring, India will probably spring to everybody’s mind. However, we believe that there are a number of other locations which have been ignored by many organisations or customers and perhaps missing out on the talent as well as economic benefit of multi-location. One such location is Nepal, which is on a similar timeline in terms of time zone for offshoring compared to India and boasts an excellent supply of superior talent. Due to the popularity of India neighbouring countries like Nepal has been completely overlooked.
